## Verdict

Steven has ~4.3 years explicit AI/ML work (research + MyCalla), which is close enough for recruiter screening. Key recommendations:

1. **In cover letter:** Frame timeline as "10 years in scientific research + 1.3 years founding MyCalla building production GenAI systems from scratch." The 10 years of ML fundamentals (neural networks, Python, data analysis) + 1.3 years of production GenAI work = demonstrates both depth and modern GenAI fluency.

2. **Position MyCalla as GenAI proof:** "Built end-to-end GenAI applications for real-world government and education clients, including deployment, monitoring, and iterative optimization — directly applicable to Citi's fraud detection and risk assessment use cases."

3. **Cloud/Docker gap:** In applications, mention willingness to upskill cloud infrastructure with team support. "Familiar with containerization concepts; eager to deepen production infrastructure expertise within Citi's DevOps culture."

4. **AI-assisted coding framing:** If asked about development process, position as "leverage modern development practices (including AI-assisted tools) + rigorous code review + production monitoring." This signals strength, not weakness.

Recommend generating PDF and applying. Citi's technical bar is high, but Steven's combination of research rigor + founding-scale execution is competitive. Risk: recruiter may reject on "5+ years" literal interpretation without phone screen. Mitigate with strong cover letter that bridges timeline clearly.
