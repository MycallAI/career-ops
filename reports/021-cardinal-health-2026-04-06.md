## Role Summary

Cardinal Health's AI Center of Excellence is seeking a Senior Data Scientist to design and deploy cutting-edge AI/ML solutions on Google Cloud Platform (GCP). The role focuses on advanced quantitative modeling, GenAI applications, and healthcare analytics to drive business decisions and improve patient outcomes. This is a leadership-oriented position requiring experience with production ML systems, RAG/LLM technologies, and vector databases.

## Fit Analysis

### Strengths

- **Python & ML fundamentals**: Extensive Python experience from UCSC bioinformatics research (neural networks, back-propagation) and MyCalla AI work directly aligns with core technical requirements.
- **Healthcare domain knowledge**: Bioengineering background (BS UCSC) provides relevant context for healthcare analytics and regulatory understanding (GxP compliance experience is a plus).
- **GenAI/LLM exposure**: Founded MyCalla with "LLM-augmented prototyping using Claude," directly addressing GenAI and vector database requirements (RAG, embeddings, prompting).
- **Advanced modeling experience**: Back-propagation neural networks and database design experience demonstrates quantitative rigor needed for advanced modeling.
- **Remote-friendly**: Candidate is in San Juan, Puerto Rico (remote preferred), and this role is fully remote.

### Gaps

- **GCP/cloud platform experience**: Role emphasizes Google Cloud Platform heavily; no GCP experience mentioned in profile. (However, cloud fundamentals are transferable, and AI-assisted coding mitigates this.)
- **Seniority level mismatch**: "Senior" title and "thought leader/architect of AI strategy" language suggests 7-10+ years of experience. Candidate has ~4 years of direct ML experience (2 years research + ~2 years founder).
- **Production ML Ops at scale**: While MyCalla involves "end-to-end" work, no evidence of deploying ML systems at enterprise scale with monitoring, CI/CD, MLOps practices.
- **Healthcare regulatory depth**: GxP compliance experience is inventory-focused, not drug discovery/diagnostics algorithm validation.

### AI-Assisted Coding Factor

- **High impact on gaps**: GCP-specific tooling (Vertex AI, BigQuery, etc.) is learnable with AI assistance. GenAI frameworks (RAG, vector DB) are well-documented and Claude can assist with implementation and debugging.
- **Framework knowledge vs. fundamentals**: Candidate has solid ML fundamentals (neural networks, data modeling); missing GCP and MLOps tooling are framework gaps that diminish significantly when AI writes code.
